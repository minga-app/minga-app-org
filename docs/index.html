What we want to solve

Situation in Chile

Only during the first months and only in Chile, Covid-19 has already left 2.5 Million people unemployed.

That means hunger for roughly 2.5 million families.

~38% of chilean families

Even Chile's so-called middle-class, which was doing well on paper but highly indebted, is losing everything they've ever had to the creditors.

The Chilean anti-foodwaste-law, approved by the Senate at the beginning of August and now waiting to pass in Congress is being rejected, and supermarkets keep destroying food.

Globally

And FAO estimates that 1/3rd of food goes to waste, emitting 6% of global GHG.
